We are the Rutgers student-run clean technology innovation contest and startup accelerator funded by the United States Department of Energy.
We are focused on connecting young, budding innovators in the green technology space through mentorship and workshops. At the end of the program, expert judges will reward the most promising company in the program with a $50,000 prize.

What will you get from this program?

  • Mentorship
  • Exclusive Events
  • Networking Opportunity with Peers and Professionals
  • A Chance to Compete for Prizes

How Does This Work?

Step 1: Complete and send your application before the deadline on F6S. Step 2: Hand-Selected Judges will officially review your application and select the top few applicants. Step 3: If selected, our mentors will help your team throughout the competition to help build your product into a business.
